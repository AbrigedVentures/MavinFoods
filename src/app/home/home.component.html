Mindtree Kalinga Campus

20 acres sustainable campus designed to facilitate software development office spaces along with training block (community of learners) and transit accommodation (convivial society). Central axis along the east west direction, from where one can enjoy the rise and the set of the sun is called as path of life, connects the whole campus. Compressed earth blocks made from the earth excavated at site are used for construction of walls. One acre of pond created in the campus is helping in achieving water neutrality.

Campus Minds

If the 20th century was the century of logical and analytical ability, the 21st century can be said to be the century of multiple intelligences. The traditional definition of intelligence is giving way to a broader definition which reflects the differing ways in which humans think, learn and act. Kalinga looks at engaging learners using different intelligences, resulting in a multi-skilled, well-rounded engineer. As a campus mind experiences this unique learning program at Kalinga, we are not just creating a programmer or a tester but someone who will engineer meaningful technology solutions to help businesses and societies flourish.
